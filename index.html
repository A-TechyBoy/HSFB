1. What are Curses and Blessings?

Curses:

  • 50% fewer doubloons.
  • Obtained by voting just to complete the task or avoiding voting by using dots to complete 10 words.

Blessings:

  • 20% more doubloons.
  • Obtained by voting and providing extra information, such as suggestions for improvement or offering more insightful votes.
